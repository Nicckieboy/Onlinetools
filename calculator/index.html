About this Web Calculator

A fast and simple web calculator for everyday arithmetic. It runs directly in your browser without installation or sign-up and works smoothly on both desktop and mobile devices.

  • Keyboard: type numbers and operators, press Enter for equals
  • Clear: Esc clears all, Backspace deletes one digit

Example: 12.5*3-4 · Safe expression parser (no eval)

Calculations are processed locally in your browser. No personal data is intentionally collected or sent to a server.
